Motorcycle

There's a lot of bikes to choose from! However, you'll have to keep some factors in mind when choosing one

  • What's your Budget?

  • Do you want to ride dirt, street or both? This will determine your riding style.

  • What would you say is your skill level? It's not recommended to start on a fast and/or heavy bike

  • Your rider height is an important factor in choosing a bike because a seat height too high for you can be dangerous
